--- layout: post title: "0.4.4 Backup Your RabbitMQ Queues" categories: news ---

QDB 0.4.4 has been released with support for automatically filling a QDB queue from a RabbitMQ queue. This makes it really easy to backup messages coming into RabbitMQ and play them back whenever you need them.

This is an alpha release of QDB. Download it and try it out but don't use it in production just yet. It works but the API is still undergoing change.
